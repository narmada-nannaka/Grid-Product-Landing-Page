Modern Packaging using Bananas

Biodegradable

Waste banana plant stalks are turned into biodegradable and recyclable shopping bags.

Reducing Waste

Only 12 percent of the banana plant is used, which is the actual fruit and the rest is discarded after every harvest.

Multiple Applications

Without much processing, banana leaves are already in use for packaging. Similarly, proposed applications include paper products, textiles and drug delivery.
